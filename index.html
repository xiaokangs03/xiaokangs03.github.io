Xiaokang Sun

B.E. in Automation, Tsinghua University

Hi, I’m a senior undergraduate studying at Department of Automation, Tsinghua University. I am seeking for a PhD/Mphil position starting in Fall 2025.

I am currently working with Prof. Jiachen Li at UC Riverside on long-horizon robot manipulation. I have also collaborated with Prof. Jianyu Chen at Tsinghua University on humanoid sim2real and LLM planning, and with Prof. Yao Jiang at Tsinghua University on tactile-based skill learning and regrasp policy learning. Additionally, I am conducting my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Prof. Xiang Li, focusing on multi-agent RL.

My research interest lies in robotics, machine learning, and control theory, with a special interest in planning and control for manipulation tasks.
